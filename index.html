Watermarks & logos

ClearCut never adds a watermark to your PNG. This app does not automatically erase embedded stock stamps or logos—those are treated as part of the foreground.

  • If a mark sits only on the background, removing the background often removes it.
  • For overlays on the subject, open Refine edges and use Erase on the cutout (and Restore if needed).
  • Edit only images you are allowed to use.

Export & metadata

Files are rendered from your preview in the browser. Original camera EXIF is not copied into the downloaded PNG.
